--- layout: default title: Benjamin Tindall ---

Ben is a Bachelor of Arts candidate in Philosophy at Middlebury College and will graduate in the Spring of 2017. Currently he is one of two Faculty Research Assistants for the Department of Philosophy, and is also pursuing a minor in Computer Science in addition to Philosophy. Ben has worked at Middlebury's Technology Helpdesk since he enrolled in the fall of 2013 and is involved with various organizations on campus including Model UN and the Middlebury Orchestra. In the spring of his Junior year, Ben studied at the Middlebury-CMRS program at the University of Oxford.

In the Summer of 2016, Ben interned at The Beacon Group doing growth strategy consulting in Portland, ME. At Beacon, he was staffed on multiple projects in their technology practice from start to finish and was involved at stages from the kickoffs to preparing final deliverables.
